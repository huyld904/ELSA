Meet ELSA

ELSA (English Language Speech Assistant) is an English pronunciation app that uses AI-powered speech-recognition technology to teach English pronunciation skills.

Our award winning technology provides specific feedback on how to improve within seconds. Listen to native speakers’ pronunciation, watch videos showing mouth movements, and keep improving with daily practice.
